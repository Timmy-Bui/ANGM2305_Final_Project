# Title
Asteroid Destroyer 

## Repository
https://github.com/Timmy-Bui/ANGM2305_Final_Project

## Description
This will be a sci fi game of a spaceship flying around and destroy asteroid to earn points or something else.

## Features
- Spaceship movement and firing
	- What it say.
- Asteroid size and spawning.
	- Different size of asteroid and spawning off screen.
- Music
	- Hoping to have music for each stuff.

## Challenges
- Adding a hp to both spaceship and asteroid and having it be dmg off based from the spaceship weapon where it was pointing at.
- Different spawning of asteroid size and moving on it own path off screen.
- percent of chance to spawn equpiment from asteroid. (Ideal)
- Different type of ship class to choose from the starting screen (Ideal)
- Having the limited amount of equipment and which be removed or added to the ship effecting the stats of it. (Ideal)
- Creating a button from pygame and other stuff. (Ideal)

## Outcomes
Ideal Outcome:
- To become a game on it own, like the play button and other stuff. To have multiple classes of ships with their pro and con, different type of equipments to be collected that affect the stats. Might be too much.

Minimal Viable Outcome:
- Having it work, each asteroid give different scores depending on the size, and basicaly how long you stay alive.
